Predicting 2023

Researched by Ash, written by AI

2023 has been a harder year to predict than most.

There are major tech trends (the rapid rise of AI) and economic trends (a recessing economy) at play that will likely be quite disruptive to most predictions.

My goal with my public letter each year is to give readers a useful model of the year to come. As such, I've stuck to high conviction predictions in the letter below.

January

2023 is sure to be a year to remember, full of surprises and milestones! In January, the 118th US Congress will take their oaths, and it is expected that the COVID Public Health Emergency will be renewed for the last time.

February & March

February is sure to be an exciting month with the Grammy awards, as well as the Super Bowl. By March, the world will hold its breath as the Oscars are awarded and new aritificial intelligence products seem to be released every day.

April

April will be a time of celebration, as the COVID Public Health Emergency is expected to end, and presidential candidates begin to announce their candidacy. India's population is expected to be recognized as the largest in the world.

June & July

June will bring further excitement, as the NBA finals begin and Apple's WWDC takes place. And in July, the Supreme Court will issue various decisions that are sure to be of interest.

October

October will be another momentous month, as the DearMoon Voyage, a private space mission to the moon, is expected to set off.

November & December

November will be marked by the traditional holiday of Thanksgiving on the 23rd, and December brings the Christmas celebrations and a ringing in of the New Year.

Altogether, 2023 is sure to be an eventful year packed with milestones, making it one to certainly keep an eye out for!
